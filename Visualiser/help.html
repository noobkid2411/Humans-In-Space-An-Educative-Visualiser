* TO GET STARTED,CLICK THE TIMELINE BUTTON ON THE HOME PAGE OR THE BUTTON GIVEN DOWN.

* YOU'LL THEN BE TAKEN ON TO CHOOSE THE TIME POCKET YOU WISH TO KNOW MORE ABOUT

* CHOOSE A SUITABLE TIME POCKET.

* VOILA,YOU CAN SCROLL THROUGH THE MAJOR EVENTS AND ACCOMPLISHMENTS THAT HAPPENED IN SPACE DURING THAT TIME.

* AND EACH ONE ACCOMPANIED BY AN INSIGHTFUL VISUAL!

* ONCE YOU'VE PEERED THROUGH A TIMELINE YOU CAN COME BACK AND EXPLORE MORE!

* THAT'S IT,HAVE FUN EXPLORING SPACE!
